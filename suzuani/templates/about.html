About SuzuAni

Your premier, mobile-first destination for Anime, Manga, and Music.

SuzuAni is a passion project built for fans, by fans. Our goal is to provide a clean, modern, and immersive platform where you can discover, watch, read, and listen to your favorite content without distractions. We believe in the power of storytelling and the incredible art forms of anime, manga, and music.

This application is a demonstration of a full-stack web app built using Python with the Flask framework, Flask-SocketIO for live chat, and styled with the utility-first Tailwind CSS library.
